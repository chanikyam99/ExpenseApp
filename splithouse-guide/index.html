SplitHouse

Build Your Own
Expense Splitter

A complete step-by-step guide to building SplitHouse — a free, self-owned alternative to Tricount with correct balances, real-time activity, and spending insights.

What you'll build

A web app for your household where everyone can log shared expenses, see who owes whom, and settle up — all for free, forever.

FeatureDetails
Multiple groupsHouse, trips, events — each independent
Add & split expensesEqual split or custom amounts per person
Correct balance calculationPairwise algorithm — no floating-point drift
Audit trailEvery expense shows who added/edited it
Real-time activity feedLive updates via Supabase WebSockets
Monthly spending insightsBar chart by category, pie chart by person
Settle upRecord payments, balances update instantly
Export to CSVFull expense history download
Invite via linkOne link, click → joined
