History & Insights

Filterable expense history grouped by month with CSV export, and monthly spending charts by category and person.

Client component. Loads all expenses, provides filter controls for member and category, groups results by month, and exports to CSV.

Client component using Recharts. Shows a bar chart of spend per category and a pie chart of spend per person for the selected month.
