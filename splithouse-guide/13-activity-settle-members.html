Activity, Settle & Members

Real-time activity feed, settle-up form, and members page with invite link sharing.

app/groups/[groupId]/activity/page.tsx — Real-time feed

Client component. Loads the initial log, then subscribes to Supabase Realtime so new entries appear instantly without refreshing.

app/groups/[groupId]/activity/page.tsx

app/groups/[groupId]/settle/page.tsx — Settle up

Client component. Lets a member record that they paid another member. Inserts a settlement and an activity log entry.

app/groups/[groupId]/settle/page.tsx

app/groups/[groupId]/members/page.tsx — Members

Server component showing all members with avatars and the invite link. The archive button is client-side.
