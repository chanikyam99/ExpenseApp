Groups Pages

My Groups list, Create Group form, and the Join via invite link page.

app/groups/page.tsx — My Groups

Server component. Fetches all groups the current user belongs to and shows them as cards, split into Active and Archived.

app/groups/page.tsx

app/groups/new/page.tsx — Create Group

Client component. Two inputs: group name and your display name within the group. On submit, creates the group and adds you as the first member.

app/groups/new/page.tsx

app/join/[inviteCode]/page.tsx — Join Group

Server component. Validates the invite code, then shows a form to enter your display name and join.
