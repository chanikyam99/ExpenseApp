Expenses

Add an expense with equal or custom splits, and view/edit/delete an existing expense.

app/groups/[groupId]/expenses/new/page.tsx — Add Expense

Client component. Handles both equal splits (auto-computed using equalSplits()) and custom per-person amounts. Inserts the expense, all splits, and an activity log entry atomically.

app/groups/[groupId]/expenses/new/page.tsx

app/groups/[groupId]/expenses/[expId]/page.tsx — Expense Detail

Shows expense details, who paid, each person's split, who added/edited it, and buttons to edit or delete.
